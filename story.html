Our Story

From garage dreams to downtown landmark

Keeping Music Physical in a Digital World

Meridian Underground Music opened its doors in 2010 with a simple mission: keep music culture alive in East Mississippi. What started as a small vinyl collection in a downtown storefront has grown into the region's premier destination for records, instruments, and music lovers.

More Than a Store

We're a community hub. Local musicians meet here. Bands form in our aisles. We host in-store performances, album release parties, and we're the official sponsor of the Meridian Battle of the Bands.

What We Believe

  • 🎵 Physical media sounds better and supports artists
  • 🎵 Local music scenes need physical spaces to thrive
  • 🎵 Every customer deserves expert recommendations
  • 🎵 Music education matters
